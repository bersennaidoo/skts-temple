About Us

Shree Kamatchee Ammen Temple is a sacred Hindu shrine nestled in the heart of Newlands West, Durban. Our temple is a beacon of spiritual guidance, cultural heritage, and community service.

  • History

    December 25, 1899

    Established in 1899 by brave migrants from South India's Tamil Nadu, our temple has a rich history spanning over a century. These visionary individuals brought with them their cherished traditions, customs, and faith, which have been lovingly preserved and passed down through generations.

  • First Prayer

    December 28, 2015

    Shree Kamatchee Ammen Temple is a sacred Hindu shrine nestled in the heart of Newlands West, Durban. Our temple is a beacon of spiritual guidance, cultural heritage, and community service.

  • Temple Renovations

    January 1, 1990

    In 1990, our temple underwent a magnificent renovation, revitalizing its structure and spiritual essence. This thoughtful restoration preserved the temple's original charm while enhancing its beauty and functionality. The renovated temple continues to be a source of inspiration, peace, and devotion for our community.
